Envisioning Chinese Cinemas in the 21st Century

展望21世紀華語電影：台灣論壇

Key visionWeb app
  • Client: Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures, National Chiao Tung University
  • Completed on: December 2013
  • Skills / Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop, Html, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Wordpress

The symposium “Chinese Cinemas in the 21st Century: Production, Consumption, Imagination” aims to promote new ways of thinking about the study of Chinese cinemas by bringing together established scholars into a dynamic network that crosses disciplinary boundaries; drawing expertise from film studies, media and communications, comparative literature, and cultural studies. The Network provides a unique opportunity to forge links not just between individual scholars but also leading research centres and institutions, thereby laying the foundation for world-class collaborative research.

「展望21世紀華語電影：台灣論壇」旨在匯聚各界知名學者以建立一個跨學界的多元網絡，交流電影研究、媒體與傳播、比較文學和文化研究等專業，促進有關中國電影研究的新思路。此論壇提供了獨特的機會，不僅讓學者之間建立聯繫，也促進研究中心與機構之間的交流，從而為世界一流的合作研究奠定基礎。



Design Concept 設計理念

Based on the original logo, the design concept corresponds to Chinese splash ink and uses Chinese red, which has significance in Taoist culture and is regarded as the color of life and eternity. The film stock also represents an extension of the development in Chinese cinemas.

設計理念延續了既有logo的設計，加入中國傳統潑墨的概念並採用朱紅色，代表傳統道家文化與永恆生命的象徵。電影膠卷亦代表了中國電影發展的延綿不絕。
