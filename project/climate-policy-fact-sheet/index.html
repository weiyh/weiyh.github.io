Climate Policy Fact Sheet

氣候談判入門手冊

Editorial
  • Client: Taiwan Youth Climate Coalition
  • Completed on: December 2018
  • Skills / Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign

To introduce Nationally Determined Contributions (NDC) and Talanoa dialogue into Taiwan, Taiwan Youth Climate Coalition (TWYCC) published Climate Policy Fact Sheet, aiming to let people know more about international climate policies, such as what role UNFCCC is playing, the importance of Paris Agreement, and the idea of Global Stocktake.

因應 2018 年展開的促進性對話機制 (Talanoa dialogue) 以及針對各國之國家自訂貢獻 (Nationally Determined Contributions, NDC) 需進行之全球盤點，台灣青年氣候聯盟產出三本氣候談判入門手冊：《聯合國氣候變化綱要公約UNFCCC》、《巴黎協議Paris Agreement》及《全球盤點Global Stocktake》，讓一般大眾透過此套書籍可以更了解UNFCCC所扮演的角色、巴黎協定的重要意義以及全球盤點機制這個新概念的產生。
